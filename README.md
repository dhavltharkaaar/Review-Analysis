# Review-Analysis
This repository contains structured data and scripts for analyzing customer reviews. The dataset is designed to support text preprocessing, sentiment classification, and business insights generation. Each column captures a specific aspect of the review and its metadata.

---
## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes the following columns:

| Column Name      | Description                                                                 |
|------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Name**         | Identifier of the reviewer or customer                                      |
| **Review**       | Raw text of the customer’s feedback                                         |
| **Cleaned_Review** | Preprocessed version of the review (stopwords removed, normalized text, etc.) |
| **Sentiment**    | Classified sentiment of the review (Positive, Negative, Neutral)            |
| **Star Rating**  | Rating provided by the customer (usually on a 1–5 scale)                    |
| **Ratings**      | Numerical representation or aggregated score of the review                  |
| **Reply**        | Response provided by the business/organization to the review                |
| **ReplyStatus**  | Indicator of whether a reply was given (Replied, Pending, Not Replied)      |

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Perform **sentiment analysis** to understand customer emotions  
- Identify trends in **ratings vs. sentiment**  
- Track **business responsiveness** through reply status  
- Enable **data visualization and dashboards** for actionable insights

**## 💡 Business Suggestions from Review Analysis

- **Improve Customer Engagement**
  - Increase reply rate to negative and neutral reviews to show responsiveness.
  - Automate timely replies using templates for common issues.

- **Enhance Service Quality**
  - Focus on areas with frequent negative sentiment (e.g., delivery delays, product defects).
  - Use sentiment trends to prioritize operational improvements.

- **Boost Ratings**
  - Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews, especially 4–5 star ratings.
  - Offer loyalty rewards or discounts for verified positive feedback.

- **Leverage Insights for Marketing**
  - Highlight positive reviews in promotional campaigns.
  - Use customer language from positive reviews to refine brand messaging.

- **Monitor Performance**
  - Track reply status to ensure customer service teams meet response targets.
  - Compare sentiment distribution with star ratings to identify mismatches (e.g., high rating but neutral sentiment).

- **Strategic Actions**
  - Develop dashboards for real-time monitoring of customer satisfaction.
  - Integrate review analysis into quarterly business performance reviews.**
